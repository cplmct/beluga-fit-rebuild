Add a first-time onboarding experience for new Beluga Fit users.

Requirements:

1. When a user signs up or registers for the first time,
   detect this via Supabase profile creation timestamp or a
   new onboarding flag stored in Supabase profiles table.

2. If first-time signup is detected, show an onboarding flow
   AFTER registration completes and user is signed in.
   Do NOT show onboarding on every app open — only once.

3. The onboarding flow should be a simple 4-slide carousel
   with the following slides:

   Slide 1 — Welcome
   - "Welcome to Beluga Fit"
   - "Structured training plans designed to help you reach your goals"
   - Calm, premium visual with Beluga Fit branding
   - Continue button

   Slide 2 — How it works
   - "Choose a plan based on your goals"
   - "Track your workouts and body progress"
   - "Stay on track with daily reminders"
   - Illustrations or icons for each point
   - Continue button

   Slide 3 — Start your journey
   - "Pick your first training plan to get started"
   - "Plans for weight loss, muscle gain, strength, and more"
   - "All plans are structured and progress week by week"
   - Continue button

   Slide 4 — Ready
   - "You're all set"
   - "Let's find a plan that fits your goals"
   - Button that says "Find a Plan" and navigates to
     the Plan Library screen

4. Store an onboarding_completed flag after the user finishes
   the flow so it never shows again.
   Store this in Supabase profiles table or locally.

5. Add a "Skip" option on every slide for users who want
   to go straight to the app.

6. Design requirements:
   - Consistent with Beluga Fit's existing design language
   - Smooth transitions between slides
   - Progress indicators showing slide position (1/4, 2/4, etc.)
   - Clean typography and spacing
   - No flashy animations or gimmicky transitions

7. Do not show onboarding on re-entry for returning users.

8. Add a way to view the onboarding again from Settings,
   labeled "View Onboarding" or "Help — Getting Started"
   for users who want to revisit it.

9. After completing the implementation, summarize:
   - Where the onboarding_completed flag is stored
   - How the first-time user is detected
   - Whether the onboarding can be re-triggered from Settings
